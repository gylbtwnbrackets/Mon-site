HOME ABOUT ME MJ'S CHILDHOOD Discography

Born on the 29 August 1958, Michael Jackson is the 8th child among 9 other siblings.
Joe Jackson (His father) was the first one to ever introduce him and his brothers to the world of music, as a fellow musician.
Joe wanted to fulfill his long lost dream of being a renowned musician and chose to do so throught his sons
by forming "The Jackson Five"
Altough his methods were rough, he managed to arrange them mutliple public appearances,
the first one on National Tv being on the "Hollywood Palace" in 1969.
The group will go on to become very successfull, mainly because of Michael Jackson's talent
which set him appart from his other brothers. Their first hit song, "I Want You Back" was released in 1979
However, being famous as such a young age comes with its downside, namely the pressure put unto him by his father,
that prevented him to have a "normal" childhood. It explains how Michael went on to have childlike interest in his adulthood
to catch up to what he missed out on.
Being the main star of the Jackson 5, Michael already released his first solo album in 1972, at only 14 years old !
This already tells us how successful he will become in later years
