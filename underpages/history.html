Its origins

As can be guessed from the word play, HIStory (His story) is kind of a retrospective on Michael's career up to this point

The first part of the album contains the best classics of the artist, while the second part contains new original songs

On an other note, this his 2nd most costly album to produce, costing more than 10 millions $, right behind "Invincble"n which Iwon't talk about.

Why do I like it

The only reason I like this album is because of "Scream" featuring Janet Jackson : The instrumentation is so weird and original yet so groovy that I can't help but bopping my head when listening to it. As for the rest of the album, I think that Michael was starting to lose in relevance, altought still talented.
