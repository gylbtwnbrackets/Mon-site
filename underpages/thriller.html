Its origins

This album is, to this day, the most succesfull album of MJ.

Released in November 1982, this time under the Epic label, it was acccompanied the next year with the famous music video of the title track "Thriller" which can be called "Legendary" when we see how it influence later artists

Why do I like it

Like most Michael Jackson listener we rarely ever listen to the album itself but rather the hits songs that came out of it. However with this album, EVERY SONG is as succesful as the other : "Thriller, "Beat it", "Billie Jean" and many other. Personnaly I really like "P.Y.T" (Pretty Young Thing) a song that I discovered through Kanye West's "Good Life" which sampled the song.
